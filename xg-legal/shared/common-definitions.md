# Common Definitions

**App / Service**: The mobile/desktop application published by the Developer.

**Developer / We**: The publisher of the App.

**Personal Data**: Any information that can identify a user directly or indirectly (e.g., name, email, device identifiers when used for tracking).

**Device Data**: Non-identifying technical data such as OS version, device model, and app version.

**Local Storage**: Data stored only on the user’s device (not uploaded to the Developer’s servers).

**Third-Party Services**: Services provided by other companies that may process data (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, ads).
