Focus Timer – Support

Focus Timer is a minimal focus timer designed for calm, intentional work.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do notifications work?

Focus Timer sends a local notification when a focus session finishes. All notifications are generated and handled on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Why don’t I see a notification?

  • Make sure notifications are enabled in system settings.
  • On iOS, the app must be allowed to send notifications.
  • On Android 13+, notification permission must be granted.

Does Focus Timer collect any data?

No. Focus Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Does the app run in the background?

The app does not perform background tracking. Notifications are scheduled locally and delivered by the operating system.
