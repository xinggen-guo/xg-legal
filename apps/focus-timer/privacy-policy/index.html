Privacy Policy

Focus Timer

Effective date: 2026-01-25

Overview

Focus Timer is a simple productivity application designed to help users focus for a set period of time. Your privacy is respected by design.

Data Collection

Focus Timer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No advertising SDKs

Notifications

The app may send a local notification when a focus session finishes. These notifications are generated and handled entirely on your device. No notification data is transmitted off-device.

Third-Party Services

Focus Timer does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Focus Timer does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

If the app’s data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
