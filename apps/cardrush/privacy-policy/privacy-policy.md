# Privacy Policy — CardRush

**Effective date:** 2026-01-20  
**Contact:** sishuiliunianltk@gmail.com

## 1. Overview
CardRush is a card game designed to run entirely offline.

## 2. Data Collection
CardRush does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.
- No account registration is required
- No personal information is requested
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used

## 3. App Functionality
The game runs completely offline and does not require access to personal information or sensitive device features
(contacts, location, camera, microphone, files/media). Any gameplay data remains locally on the device.

## 4. Third-Party Services
CardRush does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Because no personal data is collected, no special handling of children’s data is required.

## 6. Changes
If data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated with a new effective date.
