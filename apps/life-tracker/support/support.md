# LifeTracker Support

## About LifeTracker
LifeTracker is a simple and private life logging app designed to help you track habits,
write daily notes, and reflect on your routine.

The app works fully offline and does not collect any personal data.

## FAQ

**Does LifeTracker require an internet connection?**  
No. The app works completely offline.

**Does LifeTracker collect personal data?**  
No. LifeTracker does not collect or transmit any data.

**Is LifeTracker a medical or health app?**  
No. LifeTracker is for personal organization only and does not provide medical advice.
