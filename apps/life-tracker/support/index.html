About LifeTracker

LifeTracker is a simple and private life logging app designed to help you track habits, write daily notes, and reflect on your routine.

The app works fully offline and does not require account registration. No personal data is collected or transmitted.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LifeTracker require an internet connection?
No. LifeTracker works completely offline.

Does LifeTracker collect any personal data?
No. The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Is LifeTracker a medical or health app?
No. LifeTracker is intended for personal organization and daily reflection only. It does not provide medical or health advice.
